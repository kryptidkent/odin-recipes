Thumbprint Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup unsifted powdered sugar
  • 1 stick unsalted butter, softened at room temperature
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 1/4 cups al-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup white sugar or as needed
  • 1/2 cup fruit jam, divided

Steps

  1. Prep: Gather ingredients and preheat oven to 325F; line a baking sheet with silicone mat.
  2. Mix powdered sugar and butter together with a rubber spatula until creamy. Add egg, vanilla, salt, almond extract and mixx well. Blend in the flour until just combined.
  3. Portion dough into 1/2 ounce balls, roll each ball in a plate of sugar to coat.
  4. Place balls several inches apart on the prepared baking sheet, flattening lightly with fingers.
  5. Poke wells into each ball with finger or end of spoon; fill wells with fruit jam.
  6. Bake until golden brown, about 15 minutes. Rest on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to wire rack to cool completely.
