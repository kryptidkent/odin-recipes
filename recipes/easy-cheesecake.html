Easy Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 6 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • two 8 ounce packages cream cheese, room temperature
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. To make crust: combine graham cracker, confectioner's sugar, butter, and salt. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Press crumbs into bottom of 9" pie pan.
  4. To make filling: Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth on medium speed. Mix in eggs and vanilla.
  5. Pour filling into crust.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes, add a crust shield or aluminum foil. Bake another 10 minutes.
  7. Remove from ove and set on rack to cool. Once cooled, refridgerate until ready to serve.
