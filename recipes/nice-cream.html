Nice Cream

Ingredients

  • 4 large bananas, sliced and frozen
  • 1/2 teaspooon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons plain milk
  • additional toppings as desired

Steps

  1. The bananas should be sliced and frozen at least 24 hours ahead of that time. They can be frozen on a parchment-lined pan and then moved to a ziplock bag if not used immediately.
  2. Pour milk and vanilla into the blender and then add the frozen banana slices. Blend on low speed increasing to high, scraping down and repeating as needed to blend until smooth.
  3. When nice cream is smooth and there are no lumps, transfer to serving bowls and add additional toppings. If the nice cream is too soft, it can be frozen for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
